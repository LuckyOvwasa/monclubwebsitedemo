About Mon Club International

Mon Club International is a humanitarian NGO dedicated to providing relief and development services to vulnerable communities across Nigeria.

Our mission is to alleviate suffering, promote dignity, and empower communities through targeted interventions in protection, logistics, nutrition, WASH, and emergency response.

We operate across multiple locations including Damasak, Marte, Rann, Bama, and Seye, responding to urgent humanitarian needs and building long-term resilience.

Our Values

  • Accountability to affected populations
  • Transparency and integrity
  • Community participation
  • Non-discrimination
  • Do no harm
